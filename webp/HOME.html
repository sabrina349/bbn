WELCOME AT MSIMBAZI CENTRE TRAINING INSTITUTE!!!!


The MSIMBAZI CENTRE TRAINING INSTITUTE (MCTI) invites all young generation and those interested in receiving quality education and vocation training different fields to join its existing campuses in various corners of Dar es Salaam city.

The MSIMBAZI CENTRE TRAINING INSTITUTE (MCTI) are colleges established with the aim of helping youth and people of all ages to get knowledge and skills that will enable them to self-employed and to find employment opportunities in various institutions and companies.

The MSIMBAZI CENTRE TRAINING INSTITUTE (MCTI) are non-government institutions granted full-registration status by National council for technical and vocation education and training (NACTVET) to offer certificate courses in the following fields.

The MSIMBAZI CENTRE TRAINING INSTITUTE(MCTI) has more than six (6) campuses in Dar-es-salaam and coastal regions, those campuses are listed on the last page where will be easy for one customers to get/find services they need.

About Course

The Certificate in Computer Applications is a practical course whose main aim is to provide opportunity for interested candidates to develop Information and Communications Technology (ICT) skills that are becoming essential in every sector of our economy.
It also aims at meeting the challenge of preparing students as they make the transition from secondary school to university and college life. The skills required to succeed in university/college are different from those required in secondary schools and very many students find it difficult to make quick adjustments especially in the instructional methods. The programme will be of special interest to candidates who have not had any contact at all with the business world and who need to develop skills that will enable them to attain their full potential in university/college or work environment.

MCTI computer application Course FAQs

  • Are MCTI and computer application Courses similar?

    • MCTI (Personal Assistant) and computer application Courses are related but not identical. MCTI Courses cover a more comprehensive range of administrative tasks, including schedule management, while Secretarial Courses focus on specific clerical duties.

  • What are the pre-requisites required for these MCTI and computer application Training Courses?

    • There are no formal prerequisites for MCTI and computer application Training Courses.

  • What are the benefits of taking these courses?

    • The benefits of taking PA and computer application Training Courses include learning from experienced professionals and developing essential skills like time management and communication for a successful career as a MCTI or Secretary.

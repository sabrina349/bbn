  • Are MCTI and Secretarial Courses similar?

    • MCTI (Personal Assistant) and Secretarial Courses are related but not identical. MCTI Courses cover a more comprehensive range of administrative tasks, including schedule management, while Secretarial Courses focus on specific clerical duties.

  • What are the pre-requisites required for these MCTI and Secretarial Training Courses?

    • There are no formal prerequisites for MCTI and Secretarial Training Courses.
